Projects

Beat Saber Clone

A fast-paced VR rhythm game where players slash incoming blocks to the beat of the music. Designed with immersive visuals, precise tracking, and smooth motion mechanics. Features dynamic level patterns that challenge timing, accuracy, and reflexes. A polished Beat Saber–style experience built entirely in Unity VR.

Top Down Strike

This top-down 2D shooter challenges players to defeat enemies using precise aim and quick reflexes.Enemies fire back, creating a thrilling combat rhythm of attacking, dodging, and repositioning. Players collect ammo and health items to maintain momentum during battles.The game focuses on survival, skillful shooting, and real-time resource management.

Car Combat Arena

A high-speed 3D car-shooting game where the player’s vehicle moves forward automatically. A roof-mounted auto-aim gun fires at enemy cars that enter the attack radius. Players dodge incoming enemy fire and steer left or right to avoid obstacles on the road. Survive enemy attacks, avoid collisions, and fight through an action-packed highway battlefield.

Highway Rush Runner

In this endless highway runner, the player navigates a road filled with oncoming traffic. Cars, trucks, and buses charge toward the player, creating intense dodging challenges. The goal is to avoid every obstacle while maintaining speed and momentum. Points are awarded for survival time and near-miss dodges.
